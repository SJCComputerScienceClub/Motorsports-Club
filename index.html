About

The SJC Motorsports Club is a club that focuses on the functions and performance of different motor vehicles. Although the club will mostly be to look at cars and how they are used, it is open to different sports and vehicles, with an axample being motorcycles. Most of the topics that will be explored in the club are part and performance focused, looking into how each part affects the vehicle and what affects how it can function in a better way. Some topics can include tuning which may be hard to understand without a basis of technology, but if persevered through, can be learned and applied. The current goal of the club is to get a great start and keep rolling after the founder graduates, and possibly get recognized as an official SJC sport.
