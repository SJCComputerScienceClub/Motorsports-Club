About Our Club

The SJC Motorsports Club is dedicated to exploring the functions and performance of motor vehicles and their components. While our primary focus centers on automotive technology and applications, we welcome all motorsports enthusiasts, including those passionate about motorcycles and other vehicles. Our club dives deep into performance-focused topics, examining how individual parts affect overall vehicle performance and discovering ways to optimize functionality. We explore advanced subjects like tuning, which, while technically challenging, becomes accessible through dedication and collaborative learning. Our current goals are to establish a strong foundation for the club, ensure continued success beyond our founder's graduation, and ultimately achieve recognition as an official SJC sport.
